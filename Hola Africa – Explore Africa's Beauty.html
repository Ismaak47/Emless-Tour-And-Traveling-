Africa's Finest: Top Safari Destinations

Tanzania

#1 of 9 Top Rated Safari Countries

Tanzania is a top destination for seeing wildlife in Africa. It has three main areas for safaris. The famous Northern area includes places like the Serengeti National Park, Ngorongoro Crater, and Mt. Kilimanjaro. People who have been on safaris before often prefer the Southern or Western areas, known for their untouched beauty. For those who love beaches, the beautiful ‘Spice Island’ of Zanzibar is a short flight away. About Tanzania

Rates (USD)

$300 to $1500+ pp/day

Best Time to Go

Visit from June to October, or between January and February to witness the wildebeest culving season.

High Season

Northern parks: best from July to March, but can be busy. Southern and Western parks: ideal from June to October, with Nyerere being busier.

77 Tanzania Safaris
Tanzania Parks & Reserves

Kenya

#2 of 9 Top Rated Safari Countries

Kenya is the heartbeat of African safaris, home to the legendary Masai Mara and the spectacular Wildebeest Migration. Witness iconic Big Five encounters in untamed landscapes, from Amboseli’s elephant herds beneath Mount Kilimanjaro to the raw beauty of Tsavo and Laikipia. For those seeking exclusivity, private conservancies offer intimate, crowd-free game viewing. Beyond the wilderness, Kenya’s coast delivers a final surprise: white sands, turquoise waters, and the silence only paradise knows. This is not just a safari. This is Kenya. About Kenya 

Rates (USD)

$350 to $1,500+ pp/day

Best Time to Go

Visit between June to October or in the months of January and February.

High Season

Visit from June to October or between December and March.

24 Kenya Safaris
Kenya Parks & Reserves

Botswana

#3 of 9 Top Rated Safari Countries

Botswana is a top choice for luxury, eco-friendly travel in Africa’s untouched natural areas. You can enjoy the beautiful Okavango Delta by going on game drives or paddling in a traditional canoe called a mokoro. Also, places like Chobe National Park, Moremi Game Reserve, and Central Kalahari Game Reserve offer amazing views and wildlife. About Botswana

Rates (USD)

$186 to $1,152 pp/day

Best Time to Go

Dry parks are best visited from March to May, while Okavango and Chobe are ideal from June to September.

High Season

July to March (Northern circuit parks; all get crowded), June to October (Southern and Western circuit parks; only Nyerere gets crowded)

50 Botswana Safaris
Botswana Parks & Reserves

Namibia

#4 of 9 Top Rated Safari Countries

Namibia is an exceptional safari destination, renowned for its unique desert regions. The wildlife may be less numerous, but the encounters are truly special. The Etosha Pan, Africa’s largest salt flat, is a standout feature. It attracts various animals throughout different times of the year, making it an excellent spot for seeing wildlife. About Namibia

Rates (USD)

$175 to $1,188 pp/day

Best Time to Go

Visit all parks between June and October.

High Season

From July to October, Etosha tends to be more crowded.

50 Namibia Safaris
Namibia Parks & Reserves

South Africa

#5 of 9 Top Rated Safari Countries

South Africa is a leading wildlife destination in Africa, renowned for the expansive Kruger National Park, a true continental treasure abounding with diverse animal species. The colossal Blyde River Canyon, ranked as the world’s third-largest, offers extraordinary outdoor experiences, and the majestic Drakensberg mountains contribute to the nation’s breathtaking scenery. Cape Town, a bustling metropolis nestled along beautiful white sandy beaches, exudes a vibrant urban ambiance. About South Africa

Rates (USD)

$149 to $661 pp/day

Best Time to Go

Best time for all parks: May to September. Cape Town is ideal from November to March.

High Season

From October to March, it tends to be crowded during school holidays.

50 Botswana Safaris
South Africa Parks & Reserves

Zambia

#6 of 9 Top Rated Safari Countries

Zambia is a great place for safaris. It has some of the best safari guides in Africa. They lead exciting trips like game drives, nature walks, and canoe rides on the Zambezi River. These trips are safe and teach you a lot about the animals and places you see. About Zambia

Rates (USD)

$187 to $988 pp/day

Best Time to Go

May to September (Most parks)

High Season

From July to October, Victoria Falls and South Luangwa tend to be more crowded.

50 Zambia Safaris
Zambia Parks & Reserves

Zimbabwe

#7 of 9 Top Rated Safari Countries

Zimbabwe, the jewel of Africa, is famed for the awe-inspiring Victoria Falls and the ancient Great Zimbabwe ruins. It’s also a hotspot for thrilling safaris, with Hwange National Park offering remarkable wildlife encounters, including the Big Five and vast elephant herds. The more remote Mana Pools, Matusadona, and Gonarezhou National Parks are ideal for serene and intimate nature experiences. About Zimbabwe

Rates (USD)

$248 to $974 pp/day

Best Time to Go

Most parks are best visited from June or July through October.

High Season

From July to October is a good time to visit, though Victoria Falls tends to get crowded during this period.

50 Zimbabwe Safaris
Zimbabwe Parks & Reserves

Uganda

#8 of 9 Top Rated Safari Countries

Uganda, a wildlife paradise, offers diverse landscapes from lush forests to open savannahs. Highlights include gorilla trekking in Bwindi and Mgahinga, chimpanzee spotting in Kibale, and classic safaris in Queen Elizabeth and Murchison Falls. Don’t miss the off-the-beaten-path Kidepo Valley for unique wildlife encounters. About Uganda

Rates (USD)

$272 to $682 pp/day

Best Time to Go

Visit between June and August or from December to February.

High Season

Visit from June to September when it's typically not too crowded.

50 Uganda Safaris
Uganda Parks & Reserves

Tanzania

#2 of 2 Top Rated Safari Countries
Tanzania, with its three safari circuits, is one of Africa’s top wildlife destinations. The popular Northern circuit incorporates bucket-list landmarks such as Serengeti National Park, Ngorongoro Crater and Mt Kilimanjaro. Repeat visitors might prefer the remote wilderness vibe of the Southern or Western circuits. For some beach time, the ‘Spice Island’ of Zanzibar is only a short flight away.

Rates(USD)

$350 to $1,500+ pp/day

Best Time

June to October; January to February for wildebeest calving

High Season

July to March (Northern circuit parks; all get crowded), June to October (Southern and Western circuit parks; only Nyerere gets crowded)
